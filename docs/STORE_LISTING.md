## Description (up to 4000 chars)

```
Push Thru is a simple, addictive tap game: hit the button, rack up pushes, and climb the boards with friends.

HOW TO PLAY
• Unlimited — tap as much as you want. Session score, high score, and all-time pushes.
• 10 Second Challenge — how many taps can you land before the clock hits zero?
• Levels — every push earns XP. Early levels fly by; higher levels take real grind.
• Style it — pick button colors and backgrounds that feel like you.

COMPETE
• Friends — share an invite link or friend code. Add each other on your phones and compare scores.
• Groups — create a crew, share one join link, everyone lands on the same group board.
• Global boards — see top all-time pushers and best 10-second runs worldwide.

BUILT FOR QUICK SESSIONS
Open the app, mash the button, check the leaderboard, challenge a friend. No complicated tutorial — just push through.

PRIVACY & ACCOUNT
• Play as a guest online, or offline with local progress.
• You can delete your account anytime in Settings.
• Privacy Policy and Terms are linked in Settings.

Questions or feedback? Email calvin.money@gmail.com
```
